## Description — English

Plan unforgettable events with EventBliss — the all-in-one app for bachelor parties, bachelorette celebrations, birthdays, and group trips.

**Plan Together**
Create events in seconds, invite your crew with a simple code, and coordinate everything from one place. Smart templates for every occasion — from wild JGA weekends to cozy birthday dinners.

**24+ Party Games**
Taboo, Ticking Bomb, Heads Up, Truth or Dare, Bottle Spin, Impostor, Quick Draw, and many more. Play offline on one device or create online rooms for remote fun. Every game with haptic feedback and epic animations.

**Track Expenses**
Split costs fairly with built-in expense tracking. No more awkward "who owes what" conversations.

**Find Inspiration**
Browse 147 activity ideas and 56 theme suggestions — from pottery painting to cocktail workshops, from 80s disco to elegant garden parties.

**Works Everywhere**
Beautiful on your phone, tablet, or desktop. Available in 10 languages: German, English, Spanish, French, Italian, Dutch, Portuguese, Polish, Turkish, and Arabic.

**Premium Features**
- All 17+ games unlimited
- Online multiplayer (up to 30 players)
- Custom questions & categories
- No ads
- Extended question pools (5000+)

Download EventBliss and make your next event legendary!
