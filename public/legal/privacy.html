This Privacy Policy applies to:

3. General Principle

We process personal data only to the extent necessary to provide a functional App and our content and services. Processing is generally based on user consent, except in cases where prior consent cannot be obtained for factual reasons and processing is permitted by law.

5. Purposes and Legal Bases

5.1 Providing the App and its core functions

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of contract). Without processing your account and event data, we cannot provide you with the functions of the App.

5.2 Communication and support

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR for contract-related requests, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest) for other requests.

5.3 Security and abuse prevention

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. Our legitimate interest is to protect our systems from misuse.

5.4 Compliance with legal obligations

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR (e.g., commercial and tax-related retention obligations).

5.5 Push notifications

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent). Push notifications are sent only if you explicitly allow this on the first start of the App. You can revoke this consent at any time in your device settings.

5.6 Newsletter

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent). The newsletter is only sent after successful confirmation of your email address through a double opt-in process.

7. Third-Country Transfers

Some of the providers listed above are based or operate servers outside the EU/EEA (in particular in the USA). Data transfers are based on the EU Standard Contractual Clauses pursuant to Art. 46(2)(c) GDPR and additional technical and organizational measures. For US providers certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework, Art. 45 GDPR applies in addition.

8. Retention Period

We store your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes mentioned above:

  • Account data: until your account is deleted
  • Event and content data: until you delete it yourself or your account is deleted
  • Payment-related data: up to 10 years (commercial retention obligation)
  • Newsletter: until you unsubscribe
  • Server logs: usually 14 days

Upon account deletion, your personal data will be deleted or anonymized without delay, unless legal retention obligations require otherwise.

9. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Art. 15): You can find out at any time which data we have stored about you.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16): You can request the correction of inaccurate or completion of incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17): You can request the deletion of your data, unless legal retention obligations apply.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18): You can request the restriction of processing.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20): You can receive a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object (Art. 21): You can object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3)): You can withdraw consent at any time with effect for the future.

To exercise your rights, simply write to us at info@myfamblissgroup.com.

10. Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right at any time to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority regarding our processing of your personal data. The competent authority is in particular:

Alternatively, you can contact the data protection authority of your country of residence.

11. Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and similar technologies (localStorage, sessionStorage) only to the extent technically necessary for the operation of the App. A detailed overview is available in our Cookie Policy.

12. Authentication (Supabase Auth)

For login we use the authentication service of Supabase. When you log in, a session is stored locally on your device (localStorage) so you do not have to enter your credentials each time. This session contains an encrypted token and no passwords.

13. Payment Processing

Premium features may be paid. Payment processing depends on how you use the App:

  • Web (event-bliss.com): Via Stripe Payments Europe Ltd. See their Privacy Policy.
  • iOS App: Via your Apple ID account and Apple In-App Purchases. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Android App: Via your Google Play account. See Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not store any credit card or other payment instrument information.

14. Maps (Mapbox)

In certain areas of the App (location maps for agencies) we use maps provided by Mapbox Inc. When loading the maps, a connection to Mapbox servers in the USA is established. Technically necessary information such as your IP address is transmitted. Mapbox uses this data exclusively to provide the map tiles, not for tracking purposes. For more information see Mapbox's Privacy Policy.

15. Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, your device generates an anonymous device token which we register with Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) or Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send you messages. You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.

16. Native App Permissions

The EventBliss App only requests the permissions necessary for the respective function:

  • Camera: Only if you want to take a profile picture or upload a photo directly from the App.
  • Photo Library: Only if you want to select an image from your gallery.
  • Location: Currently not actively used. For future features (e.g. geo search), this will be requested explicitly before activation.
  • Notifications: For push and local reminders (opt-in).

You can revoke all permissions at any time in your device settings.

17. Newsletter

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we use your email address only to send you the newsletter. The subscription uses a double opt-in process — you receive a confirmation email after subscribing which you must click before we add you to the recipient list. You can unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link in every email.

18. Automated Decision-Making and Profiling

Automated decision-making within the meaning of Art. 22 GDPR or profiling does not take place.

19. Tracking, Analytics and Advertising

EventBliss currently does not use any analytics, tracking or advertising tools. No services such as Google Analytics, Plausible, Sentry, Facebook Pixel, TikTok Pixel, Mixpanel or comparable third parties are integrated. Should we integrate such services in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly and — where required — consent will be obtained via our cookie banner.

20. Minors

EventBliss is intended for persons aged 16 and above. Some game contents (in particular "Truth or Dare") are not suitable for children. Persons under 16 may use the App only with the consent of their legal guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16 without such consent. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will delete it immediately.

21. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss and manipulation. Transmission is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Row-Level Security is enabled on our database server (Supabase) so that each person can only access their own data.

22. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to adapt this Privacy Policy to changing legal requirements or changes to our service. The current version is always available at event-bliss.com/legal/privacy.html.
