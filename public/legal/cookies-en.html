Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Cookie Policy explains which cookies and similar local storage technologies we use on the website event-bliss.com and in the EventBliss App, for what purpose and how you can control them.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your inputs and settings for a certain period so you don't have to re-enter them on each visit. In addition to classic cookies, modern web applications also use localStorage and sessionStorage — both work similarly but are stored locally in the browser and not sent on every server request.

Currently not used. EventBliss does not currently use any analytics tools such as Google Analytics, Plausible, Matomo or similar services. Should we integrate such tools in the future, this Cookie Policy will be updated accordingly and you will be asked for your consent via our cookie banner.

Currently not used. We do not use tracking pixels from advertising networks (Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, TikTok Pixel etc.).

How can you control Cookies?

You have several options for managing cookies:

  1. Via our cookie banner: On the first visit to the website, a banner appears in which you can set your preferences. You can recall the banner at any time via the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer.
  2. Via your browser: All modern browsers offer settings for managing cookies and localStorage. You can block cookies or delete existing data. Note: If you block necessary cookies, the App may no longer work properly.
  3. Via app settings (mobile): You can clear local data of the native apps via device settings → app management → EventBliss → clear storage.

Notes about the Native App

Unlike the browser, the mobile App does not use cookies in the classic sense, but stores the above data as local app data on your device. The behavior is functionally comparable.
